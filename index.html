Egusi soup

This is a Nigerian native food mostly eaten by Igbo and Yoruba people

Ingredients used in making egusi soup

  • Egusi
  • Meat
  • Stockfish or dried fish
  • Vegs(bitter leaf,spinach or leafy green)
  • Palm oil
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Maggi
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Ache
Steps in preparing egusi soup
  1. Heat palm oil or vegetable oil in a pot.
  2. Saut√© onions, peppers, and other aromatics.
  3. Add assorted meats and cook until browned.
  4. Add stockfish or dried fish and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add water or broth and bring to a boil.
  6. Gradually add egusi powder, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  7. Reduce heat and simmer until the soup thickens.
  8. Add vegetables (e.g., bitter leaf) and cook until wilted.
  9. Season with salt, seasoning cubes, or other spices.
  10. Serve egusi soup with fufu, eba, or pounded yam.
Tips in making a good egusi soup
  • Use the right ratio of egusi to liquid for desired consistency.
  • Stir constantly when adding egusi to prevent lumps.
  • Adjust seasoning and spices to taste.
